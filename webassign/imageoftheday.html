Image of the Day

Nasa posts an "Image of the Day" page every day. Here are 3 of my favourite images:

Sun Rings in New Month with Strong Flare

According to Nasa, this picture represents "The Sun released an X1 solar flare, a powerful burst of energy, X-class are the most intense flares, while the number provides more information about its strength."

Turbulent Stellar Nursery

According to Nasa, this picture represents "The lives of newborn stars are tempestuous, as this image of the Herbig-Haro objects HH 1 and HH 2 from the NASA/ESA Hubble Space Telescope depicts."

 Jupiter in Ganymede’s Shadow

According to Nasa, this picture represents "During its 40th close pass by Jupiter, our Juno spacecraft saw Ganymede cast a large, dark spot on the planet on Feb. 25, 2022."
